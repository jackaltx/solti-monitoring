# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style

The project's documentation, comments, and communication should maintain a formal, technical, and detailed prose style. Clarity, accuracy, and comprehensiveness are paramount. Avoid jargon where simpler terms suffice, but do not shy away from precise technical language when necessary. The primary goal is to ensure clear understanding and efficient knowledge transfer among technical users and contributors.

## Design Philosophy

The design emphasizes modularity, reusability, and maintainability. Components should be loosely coupled and follow single responsibility principles. Adherence to established patterns and best practices for Ansible role development is expected.

## Quality Assurance & Testing

A strong commitment to quality assurance and robust testing is central to this project. All contributions must include thorough testing, aligning with the comprehensive testing framework already in place (Molecule, multi-level verification). Tests should cover component functionality, integration points, and system-level behavior to ensure reliability and stability.

## Documentation & Examples

Each role and significant feature must be accompanied by clear and concise documentation, including `README.md` files, usage examples, and configuration options. Documentation should be kept up-to-date with code changes.
